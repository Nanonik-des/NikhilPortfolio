About

I’m a Game Designer specializing in Systems Design, Level Design, Core Game Design, and clear, structured Documentation. My focus is on crafting gameplay that feels intuitive, rewarding, and deeply connected to the player experience. I combine creative thinking with systematic design to build mechanics that are balanced, meaningful, and fun to master.

Whether I’m designing a level flow, tuning a gameplay loop, or creating documentation that aligns the entire team, my goal is always the same—deliver an experience that is smooth, engaging, and easy for players to understand, yet exciting for them to explore. I care about how players think, react, and interact, which helps me design worlds, systems, and challenges that feel purposeful and memorable..

Tatya — Puzzle/Horror
Game Designer · GDD · Level & Puzzle Design

Game Brief

We are cursed by a Saadhu in the past, and now we have come to Tatya to ask him to free us (as Saadhu mentioned the solution to the curse lies to Tatya). Instead, he asks the player to help him escape from the place — the ropes tied magically around him. The player must explore, solve item-based riddles, and retrieve relics to free him.

Task

  • Pitched the concept to the team.
  • Designed core puzzles and item-based progression systems.
  • Created detailed GDD and level layouts for mansion zones.
  • Implemented player flow, fail states, and checkpoint logic.
  • Balanced difficulty and pacing across early to late game.
